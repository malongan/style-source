# Multi-Color Beverage Splash Ad System Style

**标签**：#VigoCookbook #Other  
**触发词**：Multi-Color Beverage Splash Ad System Style  
**适用场景**：A reusable beverage launch advertising system with four color-varied templates, built from giant white 3D typography, a diagonal hero drink pack, frozen liquid motion, dense launch-ad copy, and polished commercial product lighting.  
**比例**：16:9 / 4:5 / 3:4 / 9:16  
**来源**：Vigo AI Visual Prompt Cookbook  
**链接**：https://vigozhao.github.io/AI-Visual-Prompt-Cookbook/site/

---

## 一句话理解

A reusable beverage launch advertising system with four color-varied templates, built from giant white 3D typography, a diagonal hero drink pack, frozen liquid motion, dense launch-ad copy, and polished commercial product lighting.

---

## 核心特点

- Each case uses one strong drink-category color system rather than a generic rainbow palette.
- Large white extruded block letters remain the main background architecture.
- The drink pack is the single close-up hero object, angled diagonally and cropped with impact.
- Splash, foam, ice, bubbles, or condensation are frozen in high-speed product-ad lighting.
- Typography is bold, condensed, italic, mostly white, and stacked into tight ad-copy blocks.

---

## 完整模板

```
Create a {ASPECT_RATIO} beverage launch advertisement in the Multi-Color Beverage Splash Ad System style for {PRODUCT_CATEGORY} and {DELIVERY_FORMAT}. Preserve these style anchors: one strong drink-category color system; giant white extruded block-letter architecture behind the product; one large diagonal fictional beverage pack cropped close near center; frozen splash, foam, ice, bubbles, condensation, or liquid sheets across the foreground; bold condensed italic white stacked headline typography; polished studio beverage lighting with crisp highlights; dense retail-ad layout with strong product hierarchy; delivery-context cues that stay secondary to the hero drink. Use {PRIMARY_COLOR} as the dominant field and {SECONDARY_COLOR} for shadows, side planes, liquid tint, or packaging accents. Replace all source content. Do not use Sprite, Coca-Cola, copied Korean text, copied Z/E/R/O layout, original green can, existing beverage logo, real brand name, watermark, QR code, legal microcopy, or exact source slogan. New content: {SUBJECT} is {SUBJECT_ACTION} with {PRODUCT_OR_PROP} in {LOCATION}. Background details: {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}. Main headline text: {MAIN_TEXT}. Secondary supporting text: {SECONDARY_TEXT}. Accent symbol: {ACCENT_SYMBOL}. Styling direction: {WARDROBE_STYLE}. Adapt the composition to {ASPECT_RATIO}: 9:16 should feel usable as a vertical store poster, fridge sticker, or Xiaohongshu cover; 16:9 should feel usable as a subway lightbox, brand launch KV, or supermarket display backdrop. Use photoreal commercial beverage retouching, transparent frozen liquid motion, glossy cold surfaces, crisp contrast, and a fresh kinetic launch-ad mood.
```

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Each case uses one strong drink-category color system rather than a generic rainbow palette.
- Large white extruded block letters remain the main background architecture.
- The drink pack is the single close-up hero object, angled diagonally and cropped with impact.
- Splash, foam, ice, bubbles, or condensation are frozen in high-speed product-ad lighting.
- Typography is bold, condensed, italic, mostly white, and stacked into tight ad-copy blocks.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not use Sprite, Coca-Cola, the original Korean text, or copied brand/logotype styling.
- Do not recreate the exact green can, lemon-lime soft drink premise, 355ml label, or Z/E/R/O block-letter arrangement.
- Do not use the exact source slogan, NEW burst, legal microcopy, watermark, or brand signature marks.
- Do not imply an existing real beverage brand; use fictional clean packaging only.
- Low-sugar or zero-sugar claims are allowed only as generic fictional beverage copy, not copied source branding.
