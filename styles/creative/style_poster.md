## 核心特点

- Preserve the core visual identity: A bold minimalist editorial poster style using low-angle hero photography inside a diamond aperture, folded tan paper or canvas planes, and oversized white perspective typography printed across the lower surface.
- Shoot the subject as a real-photo low-angle hero image with wide-lens foreshortening, strong sunlight, and a bright cyan sky or clean outdoor backdrop.
- Layout: place the photo inside a large rotated square diamond aperture in the upper half of a tactile folded tan paper or linen canvas surface.
- Typography: set MAIN_TEXT in enormous white ultra-bold condensed sans-serif type across the lower half.
- Make the type feel printed onto the folded surface, split by the central crease, distorted by perspective, cropped by the poster edges, and lightly textured by the paper grain.

## 完整模板

```
Create an image in folded-diamond-perspective-type-poster-style.

Aspect ratio: {ASPECT_RATIO}.

Scene: {SUBJECT} {SUBJECT_ACTION}, interacting with {PRODUCT_OR_PROP}, in {LOCATION}. Use {WARDROBE_STYLE}. Shoot the subject as a real-photo low-angle hero image with wide-lens foreshortening, strong sunlight, and a bright cyan sky or clean outdoor backdrop.

Layout: place the photo inside a large rotated square diamond aperture in the upper half of a tactile folded tan paper or linen canvas surface. Include {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}. Create a deep central fold or crease running down from the diamond point into the lower poster.

Typography: set the main headline "{MAIN_TEXT}" in enormous white ultra-bold condensed sans-serif type across the lower half. Make the type feel printed onto the folded surface, split by the central crease, distorted by perspective, cropped by the poster edges, and lightly textured by the paper grain. Add "{SECONDARY_TEXT}" only as tiny clean editorial microcopy using {ACCENT_SYMBOL}.

Mood and finish: bold minimalism, streetwear editorial, monumental confidence, warm tan cardstock texture, crisp sunlight, cyan sky contrast, soft shadows, printed-paper grain, sharp geometric photo crop. Avoid watermarks, usernames, QR codes, creator IDs, platform logos, and app marks.
```
