## 一句话理解

A sparse retro PSA poster system where one visible class of material is arranged into a different recognizable symbolic silhouette, printed as a muted blue-green halftone object on aged cream paper with compact red-and-navy campaign typography.

---

## 核心特点

- The central idea is material-as-symbol: one category of visible source objects must be assembled into a different readable target silhouette.
- The target silhouette must read clearly from a distance before the viewer notices the individual source pieces.
- The source material must remain legible up close through repeated parts, seams, caps, folds, cables, rims, labels, or textures.
- Aged warm cream paper background with visible grain and faint uneven printing.
- One dominant blue-green halftone assemblage object, placed as a sculptural PSA metaphor rather than a realistic scene.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- The central idea is material-as-symbol: one category of visible source objects must be assembled into a different readable target silhouette.
- The target silhouette must read clearly from a distance before the viewer notices the individual source pieces.
- The source material must remain legible up close through repeated parts, seams, caps, folds, cables, rims, labels, or textures.
- Aged warm cream paper background with visible grain and faint uneven printing.
- One dominant blue-green halftone assemblage object, placed as a sculptural PSA metaphor rather than a realistic scene.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate plastic bottles, bottle caps, or clear bottle ribbing as the source material.
- Do not recreate a dog, pet, pet-friendly premise, or the original animal silhouette.
- Do not use the original headline, footer slogan, recycle-icon cluster, or any closely paraphrased text.
- Do not use the same diagonal bottle-body assembly, cap pile, loose cap detail, or two-tall-bottles-on-right composition.
- Do not let the source material become a random pile, tower, product stack, or abstract sculpture without a clearly readable target silhouette.
