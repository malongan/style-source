# 东亚复古杂志风插画

**标签**：#韩纸 #复古 #杂志 #插画 #花卉 #水彩 #线描  
**触发词**：东亚复古杂志、韩纸质感、韩国杂志插画、复古花卉、线描水彩  
**适用场景**：个人肖像插画、杂志封面、艺术海报、品牌视觉

## 一句话理解

融合韩国传统美学与现代时尚，以韩纸质感、细腻线描、单种花卉主题和莫兰迪色系，将真人肖像转为优雅复古的东亚杂志风插画。

---

## 核心特点

- **保留真人面部特征**：以参考图为基础，保持面部比例、肤色、表情
- **非直视镜头**：眼神柔和望向远方，带梦幻怀旧感
- **单一花卉主题**：每次仅选一种花（芍药/山茶/梅/玉兰/莲等），超大白花围绕人物
- **韩纸背景质感**：温暖做旧韩纸肌理，淡墨线描、水彩晕染
- **韩服改良服装**：基于韩服元素（jeogori、chima、durumagi）+ 现代剪裁
- **莫兰迪色调**：象牙白、奶油、淡薄荷、鼠尾草绿、米杏、柔桃 + 一处鲜艳点缀色
- **细腻手绘感**：精致墨线、流畅轮廓、手绘纹理、柔和水彩阴影

## 完整模板

```
Use the uploaded portrait images as the primary identity reference. Preserve the subject's real facial identity, facial proportions, calm expression, skin tone, natural features, and overall impression. Do not turn the subject into a different person.

Regardless of the gaze direction in the reference image, the subject must not look directly at the camera. The eyes should gaze softly into the distance, slightly away from the viewer, with a dreamy, wistful, calm expression.

Create a refined East Asian vintage magazine-style illustration of a graceful Korean woman, blending Korean traditional aesthetics with modern editorial fashion art. Place the subject in a poetic balanced composition with elegant negative space.

Randomly choose one natural variation for each element:
- hairstyle/accessory: choose a modern, soft, trendy hairstyle that suits the reference face, subtly inspired by Korean traditional aesthetics, such as softly parted hair, loose low chignon, natural half-up hair, simple braid, or relaxed low bun, with minimal accents like a slim binyeo, understated daenggi ribbon, tiny floral hairpin, or delicate norigae-inspired detail. Keep it contemporary, refined, and restrained; avoid ornate Chinese-style hairpieces or oversized decorative pins;
- botanical theme: select only one flower type per image from peony, camellia, plum blossom, magnolia, lotus, water lily, hibiscus, daisy, calendula, or wildflowers. Do not repeatedly default to the same flower type; each generation should feel like a different single-flower variation;
- pose/framing: three-quarter view, gentle side profile, seated pose, or close-up bust portrait with an averted gaze;
- outfit: choose a modern Korean hanbok-inspired outfit based on authentic Korean silhouettes, such as jeogori, chima, durumagi, baeja, layered jeogori jacket, ivory modern hanbok dress, pale mint layered jacket, muted peach blouse, sage-green editorial dress, or cream hanbok-inspired outfit. Include subtle goreum-like ribbons, soft collars, restrained embroidery, relaxed modern styling, and elegant fabric movement; avoid Chinese hanfu-style robes, wide translucent outer shawls, xianxia-style draped layers, fantasy sleeves, or non-Korean historical costume elements;
- accent color: deep crimson, jade green, royal blue, vermilion red, antique gold, or dark plum.

Use oversized botanical elements arranged organically around the figure, using only the selected single botanical theme for that image. Use delicate ink linework, clean fluid contours, subtle hand-drawn texture, soft watercolor shading, and a warm aged hanji paper background.

The outfit should include subtle hanbok-inspired details such as flowing sleeves, layered fabric, soft ribbons, embroidery, gentle collars, relaxed silhouettes, or elegant fabric movement.

Use a muted palette of ivory, cream, pale mint, sage green, faded beige, muted peach, dusty coral, warm gray, and soft brown ink tones, with one vivid accent color as the visual focal point.

The mood is graceful, nostalgic, fresh, poetic, quiet, and luxurious, like a high-end Korean art poster, luxury fashion magazine illustration, and modern East Asian botanical portrait. Keep the face elegant and recognizable.

No direct eye contact, no staring at the viewer, no front-facing passport-photo expression, no mixed-flower bouquet, no multiple flower species in one image, no repeated default flower choice, no Chinese hanfu-style robe, no xianxia-style costume, no wide translucent outer shawl, no fantasy draped layers, no ornate Chinese-style hairpieces, no oversized decorative pins, no text, no logo, no typography, no photorealism, no 3D, no glossy digital rendering, no harsh neon colors, no heavy anime style, no plastic skin, no excessive facial transformation, no idol-like face replacement.
```

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- 韩纸（hanji）做旧背景质感 + 水彩晕染
- 精致墨线描边 + 流畅手绘轮廓
- 单一超大花卉围绕人物构图
- 莫兰迪主色调 + 一处鲜艳点缀色
- 韩服元素（goreum飘带、柔软领口、刺绣细节）
- 眼神不看镜头，望向远方

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- 避免中国汉服/仙侠风/宽大披纱
- 避免多种花混在一起
- 避免正面直视镜头
- 避免写实摄影 / 3D渲染 / 光泽数码感
- 避免二次元动漫风 / 塑料肌肤
