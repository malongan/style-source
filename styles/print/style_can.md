# Blue Chinese Perspective Type Canyon Style

**标签**：#VigoCookbook #Other  
**触发词**：Blue Chinese Perspective Type Canyon Style  
**适用场景**：A Chinese typographic poster system built from an extreme one-point perspective corridor: a saturated blue central trapezoid plane carries stacked oversized white Chinese display type, while black side walls are packed with warped white and pale gray Chinese support copy.  
**比例**：16:9 / 4:5 / 3:4 / 9:16

## 一句话理解

A Chinese typographic poster system built from an extreme one-point perspective corridor: a saturated blue central trapezoid plane carries stacked oversized white Chinese display type, while black side walls are packed with warped white and pale gray Chinese support copy.

---

## 核心特点

- Extreme one-point perspective corridor, as if the viewer is standing inside a narrow vertical advertising alley
- Central saturated blue trapezoid plane runs from the foreground toward the vanishing point and occupies the strongest visual hierarchy
- Black left and right walls converge inward and carry dense skewed white or pale gray Chinese typography
- Huge white Chinese display glyphs or numerals dominate the foreground, cropped by the image edges
- Typography is stretched, condensed, bold, commercial, and spatially warped by perspective rather than laid flat

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate the original bargain sale premise, limited-time clearance premise, or exact shopping promotion
- Do not use the original price, the original large hour count, or the original satisfaction guarantee wording
- Do not reuse the original Chinese headline, original English side-wall phrases, original store-like naming, or exact dates
- Do not copy the exact top price block, exact center character stack, exact side wall text arrangement, or identical text order
- Do not include real brand marks, platform logos, QR codes, watermarks, usernames, or creator signatures
