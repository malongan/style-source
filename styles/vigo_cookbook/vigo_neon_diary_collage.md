## 核心特点

- Charcoal black background with thin vertical mobile-scroll framing and high-density stacked sections.
- Documentary outdoor photo-collage subject cutouts with rough white keyline edges and mixed scale.
- Acid green and white oversized condensed headline typography on slanted black label strips.
- Orange hand-drawn arrows, loops, lightning marks, route lines, and small doodle bursts around content blocks.
- Ripped notebook paper or torn tape panels used as schedule, rules, or checklist sections.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Charcoal black background with thin vertical mobile-scroll framing and high-density stacked sections.
- Documentary outdoor photo-collage subject cutouts with rough white keyline edges and mixed scale.
- Acid green and white oversized condensed headline typography on slanted black label strips.
- Orange hand-drawn arrows, loops, lightning marks, route lines, and small doodle bursts around content blocks.
- Ripped notebook paper or torn tape panels used as schedule, rules, or checklist sections.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate trail running, marathon racing, race bibs, hydration vests, trekking poles, or the original athlete poses.
- Do not copy the original Chinese title, dates, numbered section names, app logo, watermark, creator handle, QR code, or platform interface.
- Do not reproduce the exact mountain trail, grass slope, white notebook schedule, video thumbnail, or rule sheet sequence.
- Do not use identifiable real athletes, brand marks, event names, or the same outdoor competition story premise.
