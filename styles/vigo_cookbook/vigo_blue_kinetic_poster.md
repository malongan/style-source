## 一句话理解

A loud flat comic-poster system built from electric-blue grid paper, oversized neon-lime speech-panel geometry, heavy black block typography, sharp yellow back plates, radial action marks, and gritty print texture. The style feels like a kinetic street flyer where the headline is the main subject.

---

## 核心特点

- Electric-blue background fills the canvas and carries a faint square grid, like printed graph paper.
- A giant neon-lime speech-bubble or banner panel dominates the center with a sharp triangular tail or cutout.
- A flat yellow geometric back plate peeks from behind the lime panel to create high-contrast poster depth.
- Massive black block typography is the primary visual mass, cropped tight and packed inside the lime shape.
- Letterforms feel blunt, heavy, slightly irregular, and comic-like rather than refined or corporate.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Electric-blue background fills the canvas and carries a faint square grid, like printed graph paper.
- A giant neon-lime speech-bubble or banner panel dominates the center with a sharp triangular tail or cutout.
- A flat yellow geometric back plate peeks from behind the lime panel to create high-contrast poster depth.
- Massive black block typography is the primary visual mass, cropped tight and packed inside the lime shape.
- Letterforms feel blunt, heavy, slightly irregular, and comic-like rather than refined or corporate.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not use the original headline text: 再出发, DEPART, again, GOGOGO, or close variants of the same wording.
- Do not recreate the same restart, departure, leave-again, or go-go story premise.
- Do not copy the exact lime speech-bubble silhouette, exact yellow wedge placement, or exact black ray arrangement.
- Do not include the small signature-like mark from the lower-left corner of the reference.
- Do not use brand marks, platform icons, usernames, QR codes, creator IDs, watermarks, or app logos.
