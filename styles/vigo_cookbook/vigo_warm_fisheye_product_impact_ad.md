**标签**：#VigoCookbook #Other

## 一句话理解

A high-impact product advertising style built from ultra-wide close-up photography, a tunnel of textured foreground pieces, oversized angled white Chinese display type, warm amber-brown contrast, tiny top navigation labels, product-pack callouts, and compressed social-banner density. It feels like a loud commercial thumbnail shot from inside a pile of snack-like objects, with the product and subject rushing toward the viewer.

---

## 核心特点

- Extreme fisheye or ultra-wide macro perspective from inside a product pile, bowl, or tunnel.
- Foreground is crowded with many warm brown or golden textured pieces forming a thick border around the image.
- Hero subject is very close to the lens with cropped face, hand, object, package, or product detail pressing toward the viewer.
- Dense commercial banner hierarchy with little empty space, layered subject, product pack, headline, and small captions.
- Huge angled white Chinese display headline with heavy black shadow or extruded 3D block depth.

## 变量使用指南

| 变量 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| `SUBJECT` | new main subject, product presenter, tiny robot, athlete, maker, hand model, tool, device, or object hero that is not the source subject |
| `SUBJECT_ACTION` | leaning into the lens, reaching through foreground pieces, bursting from a product pile, presenting the prop, grabbing the hero product, or reacting with exaggerated ad energy |
| `PRODUCT_OR_PROP` | new package, beverage, snack, tool, device, camera, accessory, or product pack that is not the original source product |
| `LOCATION` | warm studio tunnel, tabletop pile, product bowl, snack canyon, counter scene, compact commercial set, or macro advertising environment |
| `BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS` | ring of textured foreground objects, dark warm vignette, blue accent blocks, top category labels, small product callout, motion streaks, tilted headline, and dense bottom caption strip |
| `MAIN_TEXT` | short punchy Chinese headline or exclamation, usually 2 to 5 large characters, set in huge white extruded display type |

---

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Extreme fisheye or ultra-wide macro perspective from inside a product pile, bowl, or tunnel.
- Foreground is crowded with many warm brown or golden textured pieces forming a thick border around the image.
- Hero subject is very close to the lens with cropped face, hand, object, package, or product detail pressing toward the viewer.
- Dense commercial banner hierarchy with little empty space, layered subject, product pack, headline, and small captions.
- Huge angled white Chinese display headline with heavy black shadow or extruded 3D block depth.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Domestic cats, cat faces, cat body crops, cat expressions, cat whiskers, cat tongues, or any pet-food story.
- Cat food, kibble, pet nutrition packaging, milk-cat wording, or the original product premise.
- The exact Chinese phrases, including the source headline, source slogans, and repeated source text.
- Legend Sandy, Lan Shi, blue-white brand labels, platform marks, watermarks, creator signatures, or logos.
- The same three-subject arrangement of two upper faces and one central lower face peering through kibble.
