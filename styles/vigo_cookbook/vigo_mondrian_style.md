## 一句话理解

把任何主题重新放进红黄蓝白黑的网格系统：让色块建立节奏，让黑线组织结构，让标题本身变成图形和隐喻。

---

## 核心特点

- **几何网格构图**：粗黑线条分割画面，形成严格的网格系统
- **红黄蓝白黑五色**：经典 De Stijl 配色，极简高对比
- **标题即图形**：文字不仅是信息，更是视觉元素
- **克制发力**：看似极简，每一条线、每一个色块都精心设计
- **中英双语**：中文主标题 + 英文副标题/理念标语

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- 粗黑线条网格分割画面
- 红、黄、蓝、白、黑五色为主
- 几何色块建立节奏
- 标题文字作为图形元素
- 极简主义，高对比度
- 蒙德里安风格（De Stijl / 新造型主义）

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- 渐变、阴影、3D效果
- 超过五种颜色
- 混乱的排版
- 过于写实的图像

## 示例案例

### 案例1：Her Name 品牌Logo
```
A De Stijl (Mondrian) style logo design for "Her Name", an AI women community platform. Geometric grid composition with bold black outlines and primary color blocks (red, blue, yellow). A minimalist female silhouette profile with long hair integrated into the letter "H". Typography includes large artistic letters "Her Name", subtitle "AI WOMEN COMMUNITY · CONNECT · SHARE · GROW", and Chinese text "AI 女性社群 / 连接·分享·成长". Bauhaus style, minimalist, flat design, vector graphics, high contrast, modern.
```

### 案例2：书店海报
```
Mondrian style bookstore poster, primary colors red yellow blue, black grid lines, minimalist geometric composition, flat design. Center large Chinese characters "书店" (Bookstore), left side vertical text "阅读 连接思想 照亮人生" and English "READING CONNECTS IDEAS LIGHTS UP LIFE", right side text "一家书店 温暖一座城", "BOOKSTORE", "好书 好时光 好生活". Middle section features bookshelf illustration with red books, blue books, open white book, and white book stack. Black pendant lamp hanging from top. Modern, artistic, warm atmosphere, vector illustration style, high contrast, 8k, poster design.
```

### 案例3：长期主义海报
```
A minimalist poster design in Mondrian style, featuring geometric grid layout with primary colors (red, yellow, blue, black) and beige background. Large bold Chinese typography "长期主义" in the center. Text elements include: "长期主义不是慢，而是在正确的方向上持续复利", "LONG-TERM THINKING COMPOUNDING TIME & GROWTH", "时间是复利的朋友，耐心是价值的放大器". Include chart elements showing exponential growth curve and bar charts. Vertical text "LONG-TERMISM" on left side. Clean, modern, corporate aesthetic with Swiss design influence.
```

## 设计原则

1. **网格系统** - 将任何主题重新放进红黄蓝白黑的网格框架
2. **克制发力** - 每一条线、每一个色块都经过精心设计
3. **视觉隐喻** - 标题本身变成图形元素
4. **秩序感** - 看似极简，实则精准控制
5. **主题变化，秩序不变** - 视觉语言统一，内容灵活替换
