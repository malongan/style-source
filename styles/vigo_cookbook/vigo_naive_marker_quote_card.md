## 一句话理解

A tall, hand-drawn absurdist quote-card poster system with crude black marker outlines, pale pastel panels, blocky blue lettering, off-kilter label tabs, and a simple central object-character gag rendered as flat naive illustration.

---

## 核心特点

- Skinny poster-card composition with a large pale pastel interior rectangle and rough uneven black border.
- Small off-center tab or nameplate at the top and a tiny lower caption plate, both drawn like taped paper labels.
- Single absurd central object-character taking most of the middle area, simplified into flat stacked shapes.
- Naive hand-drawn black marker outlines with wobbly contours, uneven line weight, and visible human imperfection.
- Blocky uppercase blue hand lettering arranged in short broken lines, with irregular spacing and imperfect baselines.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Skinny poster-card composition with a large pale pastel interior rectangle and rough uneven black border.
- Small off-center tab or nameplate at the top and a tiny lower caption plate, both drawn like taped paper labels.
- Single absurd central object-character taking most of the middle area, simplified into flat stacked shapes.
- Naive hand-drawn black marker outlines with wobbly contours, uneven line weight, and visible human imperfection.
- Blocky uppercase blue hand lettering arranged in short broken lines, with irregular spacing and imperfect baselines.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate a cow, cattle head, animal legs, horns, or a cow body emerging from food.
- Do not recreate a burger, bun, cheese slice, lettuce, meat patty, sandwich stack, or food-animal transformation premise.
- Do not use the source quote text, including any wording about killing a cow or making a burger.
- Do not use the visible celebrity name, quoted author name, signature, watermark, creator mark, or top label text from the source.
- Do not repeat the exact vertical text placement, exact line breaks, exact top tab wording, or bottom nameplate wording from the source.
