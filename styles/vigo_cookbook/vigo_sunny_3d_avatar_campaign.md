# Sunny 3D Avatar Campaign Style

**标签**：#VigoCookbook #Other

## 一句话理解

A bright social campaign poster style built from glossy toy-like 3D avatars, saturated blue-sky outdoor lighting, exaggerated wide-angle perspective, oversized slanted headline typography, and neon hand-drawn motion marks.

---

## 核心特点

- Glossy stylized 3D avatar rendering with smooth doll-like faces, rounded hands, soft plastic skin, and simplified expressive eyes.
- Extreme sunny outdoor campaign lighting with crisp highlights, clean shadows, and a clear vacation-day atmosphere.
- Deep saturated sky-blue upper field balanced by vivid green or fresh outdoor color in the lower field.
- Wide-angle near-camera perspective with the lead avatar or prop cropped large in the foreground and secondary elements stepping backward.
- Oversized bold white headline typography, angled slightly, stacked over the sky area with a dimensional poster feel.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Glossy stylized 3D avatar rendering with smooth doll-like faces, rounded hands, soft plastic skin, and simplified expressive eyes.
- Extreme sunny outdoor campaign lighting with crisp highlights, clean shadows, and a clear vacation-day atmosphere.
- Deep saturated sky-blue upper field balanced by vivid green or fresh outdoor color in the lower field.
- Wide-angle near-camera perspective with the lead avatar or prop cropped large in the foreground and secondary elements stepping backward.
- Oversized bold white headline typography, angled slightly, stacked over the sky area with a dimensional poster feel.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- No platform logo, app watermark, username, creator mark, QR code, or social platform search branding.
- Do not recreate the original Chinese summer-party headline or any exact source wording.
- Do not use the same camping-chair lawn scene, tent, lantern, suitcase, drink cup cluster, or identical picnic story premise.
- Do not copy the two original avatar identities, facial styling, hats, sunglasses, poses, clothing combinations, or foreground peace sign.
- Avoid palm-leaf frame placement that duplicates the reference layout exactly.
