## 一句话理解

A sparse fresh-market poster system with a warm ivory paper field, one oversized glossy produce hero, a smaller echo product, violent black hand-cut brush typography, thin editorial header text, and tiny bottom seal-like captions.

---

## 核心特点

- Warm ivory or pale cream paper background with very large uncluttered negative space.
- One oversized photoreal produce hero cropped near an edge, plus one much smaller echo produce item.
- Produce surface is glossy, rounded, bright, softly airbrushed, and slightly posterized rather than documentary.
- Hard scale contrast between produce and typography: subject dominates the lower or side field while type crowds the upper or opposite side.
- Large black headline built from torn, stamped, rough-brush CJK-inspired blocks with uneven edges and dry ink texture.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Warm ivory or pale cream paper background with very large uncluttered negative space.
- One oversized photoreal produce hero cropped near an edge, plus one much smaller echo produce item.
- Produce surface is glossy, rounded, bright, softly airbrushed, and slightly posterized rather than documentary.
- Hard scale contrast between produce and typography: subject dominates the lower or side field while type crowds the upper or opposite side.
- Large black headline built from torn, stamped, rough-brush CJK-inspired blocks with uneven edges and dry ink texture.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate tomatoes as the main subject or the same red tomato pair composition.
- Do not copy the original brand names, Mother Day wording, Chinese slogan, numbers, or bottom location labels.
- Do not use the exact top-left logo arrangement or any watermark-like platform mark.
- Do not repeat the same right-side black text silhouette or the exact dense character shapes.
- Do not make a Mother's Day campaign or sweetness slogan as the story premise.
