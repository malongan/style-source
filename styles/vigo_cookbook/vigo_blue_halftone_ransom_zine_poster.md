## 一句话理解

A rough cobalt-blue ransom-zine poster system built from torn white paper scraps, black photocopied halftone subject cutouts, marker-like handwritten words, crumpled paper texture, and sparse noisy micro captions.

---

## 核心特点

- Dominant saturated cobalt-blue poster field with a narrow off-white paper border or visible paper edge.
- Large irregular torn white paper islands, each with jagged scissor-cut edges and slight shadow or crease relief.
- Black-and-white photocopy halftone subject cutouts with coarse dot screens, high contrast, and slightly broken edges.
- Two or three oversized cutout subjects arranged as flat collage pieces, not realistic objects inside a scene.
- Handwritten marker typography on torn paper strips, with uneven baseline, casual spacing, and imperfect black ink.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Dominant saturated cobalt-blue poster field with a narrow off-white paper border or visible paper edge.
- Large irregular torn white paper islands, each with jagged scissor-cut edges and slight shadow or crease relief.
- Black-and-white photocopy halftone subject cutouts with coarse dot screens, high contrast, and slightly broken edges.
- Two or three oversized cutout subjects arranged as flat collage pieces, not realistic objects inside a scene.
- Handwritten marker typography on torn paper strips, with uneven baseline, casual spacing, and imperfect black ink.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate geese, ducks, swans, long-neck birds, bird bodies, bird heads, or the same animal-call joke premise.
- Do not copy the exact words DON'T, BE, A, SILLY, GOOSE, HONK, QUACK, CLUCK, or their arrangement.
- Do not include the same two-bird left-right layout, the same long horizontal bird cutout at the top, or a bottom-left barcode block.
- Do not use the original source's exact white speech scraps, vertical SILLY strip, or bottom GOOSE label strip.
- Do not include any creator signature, watermark, real barcode, QR code, app icon, platform mark, or username.
