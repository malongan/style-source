## 核心特点

- Preserve the core visual identity: A high-key pop poster system built from extreme fisheye photography, a convex glass-dome center, oversized orbiting typography, hot red-magenta-orange letter fills, aqua chromatic light arcs, and light analog print texture.
- Photography: build a finished high-key pop culture poster, not a sketch.
- Use an extreme fisheye or convex glass-dome viewpoint from below, with the subject centered inside a circular lens bubble.
- Keep people, props, hair, fabric, and hands photographic and realistic while allowing wide-angle edge distortion.
- Layout: keep the surrounding field mostly warm off-white and pale grey so the graphic system has open space.

| `SUBJECT_ACTION` | leaning over a fisheye lens, crouching inside a circular dome, looking down toward camera, reaching across frame, presenting a prop, or sitting in a compressed lens bubble |
| `PRODUCT_OR_PROP` | featured object, tool, instrument, product, bag, scooter, plant module, cooler, synth, sports gear, or food item |
| `LOCATION` | clean studio, greenhouse lab, indoor market, sports court, workshop, museum atrium, transit hall, or bright exhibition space |
| `BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS` | white negative space, circular glass distortion, cyan light streaks, faint radial smears, cropped typography fragments, small caption block, prism reflections, or color bleed |
| `MAIN_TEXT` | short headline or event title, usually 1 to 3 words, set as huge warped orbiting type |
