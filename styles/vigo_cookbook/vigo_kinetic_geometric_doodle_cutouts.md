## 一句话理解

A sparse, playful illustration system where a character or object is assembled from oversized flat geometric color pieces, loose black doodle lines, paper grain, and buoyant off-center motion on a warm cream background.

---

## 核心特点

- Warm cream paper field with generous empty space around the subject.
- One central figure, object, or small scene built from a few oversized geometric blocks.
- Flat vector-like shapes with subtle printed grain, dry-brush speckle, and soft sprayed gradients.
- Asymmetric kinetic pose with limbs or parts angled diagonally as if mid-motion.
- Thick black hand-drawn contour strokes only on small details such as hands, shoes, hair, wheels, handles, or motion marks.

```
Create a {ASPECT_RATIO} illustration in the Kinetic Geometric Doodle Cutouts style. Subject: {SUBJECT}. Action: {SUBJECT_ACTION}. Prop or object: {PRODUCT_OR_PROP}. Setting: {LOCATION}. Background details: {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}. Wardrobe or styling: {WARDROBE_STYLE}. Optional text treatment: {MAIN_TEXT}; secondary micro marks: {SECONDARY_TEXT}; accent symbol: {ACCENT_SYMBOL}. Style fidelity lock: {STYLE_FIDELITY_ANCHORS}. Content to avoid: {SOURCE_CONTENT_TO_AVOID}. Use a warm cream paper background with generous negative space. Construct the subject from five to nine oversized flat geometric color blocks: tomato orange, cobalt blue, magenta pink, lilac violet, mint green, golden yellow, black, and small white accents. Include subtle risograph grain, dry-brush speckle, and one or two soft sprayed gradients inside selected shapes. Add sparse loose black hand-drawn contour lines only for small expressive details and motion marks. Keep the image flat, airy, asymmetrical, buoyant, handmade, and print-like. Do not use realistic anatomy, 3D rendering, dense scenery, logos, signatures, watermarks, QR codes, or polished fonts.
```

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate the original jumping person with raised waving hands.
- Do not copy the original blue glasses face, black side ponytail, sneaker placement, or exact limb arrangement.
- Do not include the source signature, watermark, creator name, or any platform mark.
- Do not reuse the same orange L-shaped arm, blue belly circle, pink leg, and orange bent leg arrangement as a literal composition.
- Do not reproduce the original cream portrait layout with the same diagonal leap story.
