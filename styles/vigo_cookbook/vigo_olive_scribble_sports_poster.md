## 一句话理解

A kinetic flat editorial illustration style built from off-white paper, large olive-green poster blocks, oversized action figures, rough black ink contours, red marker motion arcs, yellow-green dry-brush swaths, sparse debris, and handmade screenprint texture.

---

## 核心特点

- Off-white paper ground with a large irregular olive or yellow-green block covering most of the background.
- One oversized action subject placed diagonally across the composition.
- Flat screenprint color areas with minimal shading and no smooth gradients.
- Heavy black ink silhouettes, angular brush contours, and chaotic scratch lines.
- Red marker arcs, arrows, and motion loops crossing the subject.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Off-white paper ground with a large irregular olive or yellow-green block covering most of the background.
- One oversized action subject placed diagonally across the composition.
- Flat screenprint color areas with minimal shading and no smooth gradients.
- Heavy black ink silhouettes, angular brush contours, and chaotic scratch lines.
- Red marker arcs, arrows, and motion loops crossing the subject.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Soccer or football as the main subject.
- Overhead kick, bicycle kick, or falling soccer pose.
- Soccer ball, goal, pitch markings, stadium scene, or team uniform copied from the source.
- Red-and-white jersey plus blue shorts combination.
- A ball floating above a raised leg.
