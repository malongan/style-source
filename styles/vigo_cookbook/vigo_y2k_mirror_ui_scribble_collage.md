## 一句话理解

A gritty Y2K photomontage style that combines wide-angle flash photography, floating desktop-like interface fragments, thick electric-blue marker outlines, hand-drawn graffiti lettering, and dense analog noise into a chaotic personal-screen collage.

---

## 核心特点

- A wide-angle flash-photo base layer with exaggerated close foreground scale and compressed room depth.
- Very high visible grain, digital noise, sharpened edges, and slightly dirty scan texture across the whole image.
- A dense collage layout where photo fragments, translucent UI windows, pop-up cards, and small panels float over the scene.
- One thick electric-blue hand-drawn outline or route line wraps around the main subject or cuts through the composition.
- Large white graffiti-like handwritten headline lettering sits near an upper corner, irregular and brushy rather than typographic.

---

## 完整模板

```
Create a {ASPECT_RATIO} gritty Y2K flash-photo UI collage in the Y2K Mirror UI Scribble Collage Style. New content: {SUBJECT} is {SUBJECT_ACTION} with {PRODUCT_OR_PROP} in {LOCATION}. Supporting scene and collage details: {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}. Wardrobe and styling: {WARDROBE_STYLE}. Use a real photographic wide-angle phone-camera base with hard flash, blown highlights, reflective surfaces, heavy grain, sharpened digital noise, rough scan texture, and casual off-axis cropping. Layer fictional, unbranded desktop/mobile-like UI fragments over the photo: translucent rounded panels, tiny image thumbnails, simplified alert cards, small blue action buttons, and generic microcopy reading {SECONDARY_TEXT}. Add a large irregular white hand-drawn graffiti headline reading {MAIN_TEXT}, plus a thick electric-blue marker outline or path wrapping the subject, and include the accent symbol {ACCENT_SYMBOL} as a small drawn/sticker mark. Preserve these style anchors: {STYLE_FIDELITY_ANCHORS}. Change the content and avoid these source details: {SOURCE_CONTENT_TO_AVOID}. No real logos, no real app icons, no watermark, no QR code, no copied names, no recognizable celebrity, no exact source layout.
```

## 变量使用指南

| 变量 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| `SUBJECT` | new central person, object, or activity anchor |
| `SUBJECT_ACTION` | changed action or pose for the central subject |
| `PRODUCT_OR_PROP` | new prop or product-like object that drives the story |
| `LOCATION` | changed interior or urban environment |
| `BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS` | supporting collage objects, panels, UI fragments, and texture details |
| `MAIN_TEXT` | large expressive handwritten headline text |

---

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- A wide-angle flash-photo base layer with exaggerated close foreground scale and compressed room depth.
- Very high visible grain, digital noise, sharpened edges, and slightly dirty scan texture across the whole image.
- A dense collage layout where photo fragments, translucent UI windows, pop-up cards, and small panels float over the scene.
- One thick electric-blue hand-drawn outline or route line wraps around the main subject or cuts through the composition.
- Large white graffiti-like handwritten headline lettering sits near an upper corner, irregular and brushy rather than typographic.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate a young man taking a phone mirror selfie in a bathroom or elevator-like room.
- Do not use the exact phone, toothbrush, striped shirt, headset, AirDrop card, Finder folder window, dock, or puzzle captcha layout.
- Do not copy the original handwritten names, studio signature, January calendar, app icons, brand marks, or operating-system logos.
- Do not reproduce the same blue outline silhouette path, same character pose, same mirror crop, or same right-side stacked panels.
- Do not create a recognizable real person, celebrity, username, watermark, QR code, or platform-specific logo.
