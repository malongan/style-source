## 一句话理解

A dense flat poster system built from giant black display type, chunky white mascot figures, thick cartoon outlines, tilted cyan or mint color panels, compact badges, tiny red accents, and a clean off-white print surface.

---

## 核心特点

- Poster-first composition with an oversized black headline occupying the top band, side band, or dominant title block.
- High layout density with very little empty space, while all shapes remain simple, flat, and readable.
- One or two rounded white mascot-like figures or simplified graphic subjects drawn with very thick black outlines and minimal facial marks.
- One large tilted cyan, teal, mint, or lemon color panel crossing the middle of the frame as the main graphic mass.
- Secondary green, red, black, or pale color blocks sit behind the subject and are edged by heavy black strokes.

## 完整模板

```
Create a {ASPECT_RATIO} image in the "Bold Block Mascot Poster Style" visual style.

Subject: {SUBJECT}
Action: {SUBJECT_ACTION}
Prop/Product: {PRODUCT_OR_PROP}
Location: {LOCATION}
Background: {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}
Main text: {MAIN_TEXT}
Secondary text: {SECONDARY_TEXT}
Accent symbol: {ACCENT_SYMBOL}
Wardrobe/styling: {WARDROBE_STYLE}

Apply these style rules:
- Dense flat editorial poster composition with oversized ultra-heavy black display typography as a structural block.
- A single large tilted cyan, mint, teal, or lemon panel crosses the center, backed by a secondary inset shape or project-card block.
- One chunky rounded white mascot-like subject or simplified graphic object with thick uniform black outline, minimal face marks, and sticker anatomy.
- Off-white paper base, black linework, cyan/mint/lemon panels, and one small red or high-contrast accent only.
- Flat raster illustration with screenprint sharpness, subtle paper grain, hard fills, and no realistic light or depth.
- Add one compact speech bubble, stamp, badge, red card cluster, or editorial label accent, but never a real logo.
- Use short graphic typography only; headline text can be English or Chinese, but it must behave as a poster shape.

Important content rule: match the extracted style first, then change the source content. Use new subjects, new scene details, new props, and new text while preserving the style fidelity anchors. Avoid coffee themes, source slogans, course identities, watermarks, UI screenshots, real brand marks, exact source mascot anatomy, realistic 3D rendering, glossy product photography, anime scenes, painterly detail, long paragraphs, and cluttered background scenery.
```

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Poster-first composition with an oversized black headline occupying the top band, side band, or dominant title block.
- High layout density with very little empty space, while all shapes remain simple, flat, and readable.
- One or two rounded white mascot-like figures or simplified graphic subjects drawn with very thick black outlines and minimal facial marks.
- One large tilted cyan, teal, mint, or lemon color panel crossing the middle of the frame as the main graphic mass.
- Secondary green, red, black, or pale color blocks sit behind the subject and are edged by heavy black strokes.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- The exact white cloud-like reference character with the I+I face and raised arm.
- The reclining figure hidden under the cyan blanket or canopy from the source image.
- Coffee, coffee cups, coffee icons, or a no-coffee story premise.
- The original design-company or design-studio headline and source speech-bubble wording.
- OFFF Course logo, course badges, platform marks, creator IDs, watermarks, QR codes, app screenshots, or UI windows.
