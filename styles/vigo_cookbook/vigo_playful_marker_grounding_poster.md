## 一句话理解

A naive hand-drawn poster style built from cream paper margins, loose marker-scribble color blocks, thick uneven keylines, oversized casual lettering, and simple mascot-like figures arranged with clear public-service poster hierarchy.

---

## 核心特点

- Cream poster-paper border with the artwork sitting inside a slightly inset field rather than bleeding to the edge.
- Loose childlike marker-scribble background blocks, mostly bright sky cyan and sour lime green, with uneven white gaps.
- Flat illustration with no realistic depth, no cast shadows, no gradients, and no polished vector smoothing.
- Thick imperfect black or dark-brown outlines with variable pressure, rounded ends, and visibly wobbly hand-drawn edges.
- One oversized simple subject dominates the lower middle of the poster, with tiny supporting props or characters as scale contrast.

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Cream poster-paper border with the artwork sitting inside a slightly inset field rather than bleeding to the edge.
- Loose childlike marker-scribble background blocks, mostly bright sky cyan and sour lime green, with uneven white gaps.
- Flat illustration with no realistic depth, no cast shadows, no gradients, and no polished vector smoothing.
- Thick imperfect black or dark-brown outlines with variable pressure, rounded ends, and visibly wobbly hand-drawn edges.
- One oversized simple subject dominates the lower middle of the poster, with tiny supporting props or characters as scale contrast.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate a white human-shaped figure lying or spreading limbs across grass.
- Do not show a dog, puppy, or pet leaping toward the main figure.
- Do not use the premise of touching the ground, touching grass, grounding, or outdoor grass contact.
- Do not reuse the original Chinese or English text, dates, slogan structure, or any close translation of it.
- Do not copy the exact diagonal body pose, outstretched fingers, limb silhouette, or source layout proportions.
