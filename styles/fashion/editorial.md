# Sunlit Supermodel Nameplate Editorial

**标签**：#VigoCookbook #Other  
**触发词**：Sunlit Supermodel Nameplate Editorial  
**适用场景**：A warm analog fashion editorial poster style combining an outdoor supermodel photograph, lush foreground texture, a small material nameplate detail, and clean white lower-third title/caption typography.  
**比例**：16:9 / 4:5 / 3:4 / 9:16

## 一句话理解

A warm analog fashion editorial poster style combining an outdoor supermodel photograph, lush foreground texture, a small material nameplate detail, and clean white lower-third title/caption typography.

---

## 核心特点

- Low-angle or seated outdoor fashion editorial composition with the model filling the middle third.
- Warm direct sunlight, soft analog shadow rolloff, natural skin highlights, and slight film contrast.
- Dense botanical or environmental foreground blur crossing the lower frame.
- Shallow depth of field with garden, greenhouse, terrace, or coastal texture melting into bokeh.
- Calm supermodel posture with elongated neck, relaxed hands, and composed expression.

---

## 完整模板

```
Create a {ASPECT_RATIO} premium analog fashion editorial poster photograph. Subject: {SUBJECT}. Action and pose: {SUBJECT_ACTION}. Wardrobe and styling: {WARDROBE_STYLE}. Product or prop: {PRODUCT_OR_PROP}, with a small physical nameplate, plaque, tag, or engraved label integrated into the object. Location: {LOCATION}. Background and foreground details: {BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS}. Preserve these style fidelity anchors: {STYLE_FIDELITY_ANCHORS}. Avoid this source content: {SOURCE_CONTENT_TO_AVOID}. Use warm direct sunlight, shallow depth of field, foreground botanical or textile blur, slight film grain, soft analog shadows, tactile fabric and metal textures, relaxed luxury mood, and a close low editorial camera angle. Add clean white lower-third typography: large uppercase headline text reading "{MAIN_TEXT}" followed by the accent "{ACCENT_SYMBOL}", with a smaller white caption block reading "{SECONDARY_TEXT}". The typography should feel printed on a fashion magazine prompt-card poster, not like a watermark or app UI. Do not use real logos or real brand marks.
```

## 变量使用指南

| 变量 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| `SUBJECT` | main supermodel or editorial fashion subject |
| `SUBJECT_ACTION` | poised seated, reclining, leaning, or kneeling action |
| `PRODUCT_OR_PROP` | luxury prop, case, chair, trunk, compact, garment detail, or object carrying a small material nameplate |
| `LOCATION` | outdoor or greenhouse editorial environment |
| `BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS` | botanical or environmental details that create soft foreground and background depth |
| `MAIN_TEXT` | large lower-third editorial headline |

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Low-angle or seated outdoor fashion editorial composition with the model filling the middle third.
- Warm direct sunlight, soft analog shadow rolloff, natural skin highlights, and slight film contrast.
- Dense botanical or environmental foreground blur crossing the lower frame.
- Shallow depth of field with garden, greenhouse, terrace, or coastal texture melting into bokeh.
- Calm supermodel posture with elongated neck, relaxed hands, and composed expression.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate the curly-haired woman in a beige suit from the reference.
- Do not use the oversized orange leather handbag as the central prop.
- Do not use the words MID JOURNEY or the exact prompt-card copy from the reference.
- Do not copy the same seated pose, garden flower arrangement, or orange prop placement.
- Do not include watermarks, platform logos, creator marks, QR codes, or signature branding.
