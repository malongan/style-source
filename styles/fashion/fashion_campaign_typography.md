## 一句话理解

高端时尚 campaign 排版海报：排版本身就是画面结构，超大字体 + 强视觉层级 + 品牌调性。

---

## 核心特点

- 排版成为画面结构，不只是装饰
- 超大字体 + 强视觉层级
- 编辑式构图 + 负空间
- 真实时尚摄影 + 精致打光
- 清晰的品牌调性

---

## 四种方向详细模板

### 方向1：Surreal typography poster（超现实排版）

```
Direction: surreal typography poster
方向说明: 超大切割字母、镜像/翻转/叠加、深色奢华调性

主模板: Create a surreal typography poster, oversized cut-up letterforms, mirror and flip effects, layered typography, dark luxury mood. Theme: {主题}. Brand: {品牌名}. Main headline: {主标题}. Subheadline: {副标题}. Color palette: {配色}. The typography becomes the structure and art itself, not just decoration. Use oversized letters, dramatic cuts, reflections and layering to create visual intrigue.

风格关键词: surreal typography, cut-up letterforms, mirror flip, layered text, dark luxury, dramatic composition
```

---

### 方向2：Product hero campaign（产品主图）

```
Direction: product hero campaign
方向说明: 产品和模特同等重要、高端campaign排版

主模板: Create a product hero campaign poster, product and model equally important, premium campaign typography. Theme: {主题}. Brand: {品牌名}. Product: {产品描述}. Model: {模特描述}. Main headline: {主标题}. Subheadline: {副标题}. Color palette: {配色}. The product is hero, the model supports, typography is bold and editorial. Use strong visual hierarchy, negative space, refined lighting on both product and model.

风格关键词: product hero, campaign typography, model and product balance, luxury editorial, strong hierarchy, negative space
```

---

### 方向3：Dark fashion week poster（时装周海报）

```
Direction: dark fashion week poster
方向说明: 深色背景、强烈姿势、大字标题、高端活动

主模板: Create a dark fashion week poster, dramatic dark background, strong model pose, bold event typography. Theme: {主题}. Brand/event: {品牌/活动名}. Model: {模特描述}. Main headline: {主标题}. Subheadline: {副标题}. Color palette: {配色}. Use deep blacks, dramatic lighting, confident model pose, oversized typography. The poster should feel like an exclusive fashion week invitation.

风格关键词: dark fashion week, dramatic dark background, strong pose, bold typography, exclusive event, cinematic lighting
```

---

### 方向4：Beauty close-up cover（美妆封面）

```
Direction: beauty close-up cover
方向说明: 面部特写、精致肤质、柔和打光、杂志封面感

主模板: Create a beauty close-up cover, close-up face or profile, refined skin texture, soft lighting, editorial cover feel. Theme: {主题}. Brand: {品牌名}. Subject: {面部/特写描述}. Main headline: {主标题}. Subheadline: {副标题}. Color palette: {配色}. Use macro lens feel, flawless skin texture, soft diffused lighting, minimal typography. The beauty is the hero, text is minimal and positioned to not distract.

风格关键词: beauty close-up, face profile, refined skin texture, soft lighting, macro lens, editorial cover, minimal text
```
