## 一句话理解

A polished outdoor high-fashion editorial photography style built from low-angle architectural framing, hard Mediterranean daylight, warm stone neutrals, elongated model silhouettes, restrained luxury styling, and fine graphic lines from wires, railings, shadows, or facade geometry. It recreates the premium fashion-campaign feeling without copying the original models, wardrobe, plaza, prop placement, or poses.

---

## 核心特点

- Outdoor luxury fashion editorial photography with believable high-end campaign realism.
- Low camera position or slight wide-angle perspective that elongates legs, coats, and architectural scale.
- Warm stone, concrete, plaster, or masonry surfaces used as large tactile foreground or side planes.
- Hard sun, high sky exposure, crisp cast shadows, and a controlled warm lens flare or glint.
- Elegant neutral palette led by ivory, sand, taupe, stone gray, black, camel, tobacco, and muted metallics.

## 变量使用指南

| 变量 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| `SUBJECT` | main fashion model, pair of models, or editorial subject |
| `SUBJECT_ACTION` | main pose, stance, gesture, or movement |
| `PRODUCT_OR_PROP` | luxury accessory, travel object, sculptural object, or restrained prop |
| `LOCATION` | architectural outdoor setting, plaza, gallery, resort, terminal, or civic facade |
| `BACKGROUND_ELEMENTS` | secondary architecture, sky, wires, railings, shadows, texture, and depth cues |
| `MAIN_TEXT` | internal editorial title or mood label, not visible as overlay text |

## 风格锚点（必须保持）

- Outdoor luxury fashion editorial photography with believable high-end campaign realism.
- Low camera position or slight wide-angle perspective that elongates legs, coats, and architectural scale.
- Warm stone, concrete, plaster, or masonry surfaces used as large tactile foreground or side planes.
- Hard sun, high sky exposure, crisp cast shadows, and a controlled warm lens flare or glint.
- Elegant neutral palette led by ivory, sand, taupe, stone gray, black, camel, tobacco, and muted metallics.

---

## 🚫 避免风格

- Do not recreate the exact two women, faces, makeup, expressions, body silhouettes, or pose relationship from the source.
- Do not copy the beige cape trench, white sleeveless vest, beige wide-leg trousers, black pointed pumps, or exact wardrobe combination.
- Do not reuse the same stone column, tram-wire plaza, old building facade, graffiti wall, suitcase placement, or urban corner composition.
- Do not duplicate the left model leaning against the column with one bent leg or the right model holding a coat with palm raised.
- Do not include real fashion brand logos, monograms, store names, watermarks, creator IDs, QR codes, or social media UI.
