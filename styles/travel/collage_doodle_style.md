## 一句话理解

A bright travel-photo collage style that overlays giant flat 2D cartoon travelers, fantastical folk-art fish, black marker loops, splash marks, and comic starbursts onto realistic landmark photography.

---

## 核心特点

- Preserve the core visual identity: A bright travel-photo collage style that overlays giant flat 2D cartoon travelers, fantastical folk-art fish, black marker loops, splash marks, and comic starbursts onto realistic landmark photography.
- Photo base: use a realistic bright travel photograph with clear natural daylight, postcard-like scenic depth, readable sky and water or architecture, and a believable tourist-view perspective.
- Illustration layer: overlay one oversized flat 2D illustrated subject across the foreground and middle ground.
- The subject should look pasted or screen-printed on top of the photo, with opaque flat color fills, thick black hand-drawn outlines, simplified hands or face, exaggerated scale, playful anatomy, and bold graphic wardrobe colors.
- Doodle layer: add ACCENT_SYMBOL plus loose black marker loops, ink starbursts, blue water drops, curved strings, short splash dashes, and motion ticks as an independent graphic layer.
